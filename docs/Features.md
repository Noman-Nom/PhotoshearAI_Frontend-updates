# Features

## Authentication & Access
- login: Sign in flow for existing users.
- signup: New user registration flow.
- verify-otp: Email OTP verification for new registrations.
- forgot-password: Password reset flow via email OTP.
- accept-invitation: Accept invitation links to join a workspace/team.

## Workspaces & Team
- workspaces: Workspace selection and management hub.
- team: Team members, roles, and invitations.
- settings: Account and workspace settings management.

## Events & Galleries
- create-event: Create a new event with details and settings.
- my-events: List and manage events owned by the user.
- event-details: Event overview with collections and media.
- share-event: Generate share links, QR codes, and PIN access settings.
- guest-access: Guest access flow with PIN and OTP.
- guest-gallery: Guest-facing gallery with downloads.
- client-access: Client access flow with selection/full modes.
- client-gallery: Client-facing gallery and selection experience.
- media-view: Detailed media viewer for event, guest, or client contexts.

## Calendars
- calendar: Global calendar view of events.
- studio-calendar: Studio-specific calendar view.

## Branding
- branding: Manage brand presets for watermarking and styling.

## Analytics
- analytics: Usage and performance analytics dashboards.

## Dashboard
- dashboard: Home overview with key stats and shortcuts.

## Utilities / Internal
- email-simulation: Simulated email inbox for OTP and notifications.
